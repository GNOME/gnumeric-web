Gnumeric 1.3.92 aka "Release Candidate 1" is now available.

With this release we have gone through lots of old bug reports and fixed a large number of little things that should make Gnumeric a more polished application.

People with broken printer configurations -- you know who you are -- will take delight in the delayed initialization of Gnome Print. A broken CUPS configuration will no longer cause a hang on startup.
