    (For those worrying over the pile of accuracy fixes above it should be noted that prior versions of Gnumeric were already pretty good, certainly far better than Excel. Most of the fixes above are for extreme-case arguments where most other software packages will give totally incorrect answers and/or take extremely long time to produce any answer at all. We are slowly feeding some of these fixes back to, say, the R Project.)
