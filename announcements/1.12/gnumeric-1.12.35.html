Gnumeric 1.12.35 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.12.35.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • Work around Gtk+ icon regression.
  • Improvements to BESSELJ and BESSELY.

Details of changes by contributor:

  • Jean
    • Fix cell border style selectors with recent gdk-pixbuf. [Bug 781988].
  • Morten
    • Test suite improvements.
    • Improve relative accuracy of BESSELJ and BESSELY.
    • New function REDUCEPI.
    • Fix further fallout from glpk format change. [Bug 783077].
