Gnumeric 1.12.43 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.12.43.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • Install headers correctly for out-of-tree plugins.
  • Compilation fix for psiconv plugin

If you have 1.12.42 working for you, there is little reason to grab this version.
