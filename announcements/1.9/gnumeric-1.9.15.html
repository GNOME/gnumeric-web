Gnumeric 1.9.15 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.9.15. This release requires the concurrently released goffice 0.7.15.

Noteworthy news in this release:

  • A number of functions were added for OpenOffice compatibility.
  • Display coordinates as tooltip over graphs.
  • Sheet objects now export to xls.
  • New plugin for Hebrew dates.
  • New plugin for Christian holidays.
  • Solver cleanups. (Incomplete.)

Attention packagers: goffice no longer depends on libgnome, libgnomeui, libglade, and gnome-vfs. (To get the full benefit of this, make sure libgsf is not compiled to require gnome-vfs and bonobo.)

Attention packagers: please do not ship goffice with equation enabled.
