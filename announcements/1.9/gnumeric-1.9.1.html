Gnumeric 1.9.1 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.9.1.

This release is a development release with lots of bugfixes. Sum1 has continued his quest of throwing garbage at Gnumeric and seeing what sticks.
