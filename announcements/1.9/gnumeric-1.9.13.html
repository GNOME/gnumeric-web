Gnumeric 1.9.13 aka "TBD" is now available.

The Gnumeric Team is pleased to announce the availability of Gnumeric version 1.9.13. This release requires the concurrently released goffice 0.7.13.

The most notable changes are:

  • We continue fixing fallout from 1.9.11's change of canvas.
  • Reduced memory usage. (0% for small sheets; 75%+ for very large sheets.)

Attention packagers: goffice no longer depends on libgnome, libgnomeui, libglade, and gnome-vfs. (To get the full benefit of this, make sure libgsf is not compiled to require gnome-vfs and bonobo.)

Attention packagers: please do not ship goffice with equation enabled.
