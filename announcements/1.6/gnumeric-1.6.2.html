Gnumeric 1.6.2 aka "TBD" is now available.

This is a maintenance release consisting mostly of fixes backported from the development series.

The majority of the fixes address compatibility with Excel in cases that are somewhat extreme. For example, the value of =LEFT("x",2^32) now is "x".
